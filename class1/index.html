Homework 1

Homework 1 is alot of busy work. The are three parts to the homework. The first part is to create some pages and link them together. This part1 of the homework is just to get familar with simple html tags. Part2 of the homework is to explore CSS. In this example, the student is to look at a site layout and given some standard HTML make it look like the original. Resources are given and the exploration is more important than the final product. Finally, the third part of the homework is to create a javascript calculator. On the surface, this assignmen seems tough. But it's really all about css and getting access to and changing the page.
